Josué's Recipe for the Best Gallo Pinto

Ingredients

For the gallo pinto:

  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 2 cups cooked black beans
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • Lizano sauce to taste
  • 1 teaspoon fresh cilantro, chopped

To serve (traditional):

  • 4 fried or scrambled eggs
  • 4 portions of fresh cheese
  • 4 slices of fried ripe plantain
  • ¼ cup sour cream

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil or lard in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Sauté the onion, garlic, and bell pepper until tender (about 5 minutes).
  3. Add the black beans with about ½ cup of their broth. Mix well.
  4. Add Lizano sauce to taste and the chopped cilantro. Cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add the cooked rice, mixing carefully with the beans and vegetables.
  6. Cook over medium-low heat for 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the gallo pinto is slightly crispy on the bottom.
  7. Serve hot with eggs, tortillas, fresh cheese, fried ripe plantain, and sour cream.
